Are my login credentials safe?

Very. You never gave it to the website. You logged-in on the Samsung SmartThings website and allowed this website limited access to your account and devices. You can revoke the access anytime.

How do I permanently revoke the access I gave?

Very simple, visit https://account.smartthings.com and login. Then click on "My Locations" and under "Installed SmartApps" column, click on "smartapps" for your Hub location. Then click on "Edit", scroll down to "Things UI" under "Other" and click "Uninstall". That's it. Sorry to see you go :(
